Biography

A little insight into me and my history with music!

I first started playing guitar when I was 7 years old, starting out with classical and focusing mainly on spanish flamenco. When I turned 11, I discovered rock and metal music, which ultiamtely lead to me getting my first electric guitar for my birthday that year. Since then, I have primarily played electric guitar as my first choice of instrument. I spent the first few years of owning an electric trying to improve and learn the different techniques used in heavier music. When I was 15 I started my first band, "Shadows of the Sun". Whilst we never went on to play any shows or release any material, I very much enjoyed the writing aspect and playing music with friends. I then went on to join an Arctic Monkeys cover band in my late teens where we played a few shows, before taking a break from music for almost 5 years. When lockdown hit and I was suddenly hit with lots of spare time, my desire and urge to write music returned, and since then I have been very busy working on music. My main 3 projects the last 4 years have been LaVein, Convulsion and Garsenda, and a couple of other smaller projects that are still being worked on. I have managed to release multiple EPs and singles in this time, shared the stage with some of my idols, and played shows across the UK and Ireland.
